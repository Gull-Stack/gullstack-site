About St. Joseph Villa

Our Story

For over 60 years, St. Joseph Villa has been a cornerstone of senior care in Salt Lake City. What began as a simple mission to care for the aging has grown into a premier senior living campus that serves hundreds of families every year.

Located on Bishop Federal Lane, our campus brings together skilled nursing, post-acute rehabilitation, assisted living, and behavioral health services ‚Äî all in one place. This means your loved one never has to leave the community they know and trust, no matter how their care needs change.

We are part of the Ensign Services family, which gives us access to the resources, training, and support of one of the nation's leading healthcare organizations ‚Äî while maintaining the intimate, family-like atmosphere that makes St. Joseph Villa special.
