The Challenge

Agile Counseling is a therapy practice in Sandy, Utah run by Candice Gonzalez, LCSW — a licensed clinical social worker with 22 years of experience. She serves individuals, children, and families across the Salt Lake Valley.

The existing website had just 4 pages: home, about, contact, and resources. While the content was professional, the site was missing critical SEO infrastructure to capture local search traffic.

People searching for "therapist in Draper" or "child counselor South Jordan" would never find her — even though she actively serves those communities.
