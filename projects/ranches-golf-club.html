Bringing a Website Up to Par

Utah's mountains deserved better than an outdated website.

The Ranches Golf Club makes stunning use of the natural hills and desert landscape of Utah's mountains. But their website wasn't doing the course justice. Golfers checking the site on their phones couldn't navigate it. The booking process was clunky. Events weren't easy to find or register for.

They needed a mobile-first redesign that showcased the beauty of the course while making it easy for golfers to book tee times, buy season passes, and register for tournaments.
